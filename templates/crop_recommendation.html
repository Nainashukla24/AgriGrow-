How It Works

Our crop recommendation system uses soil parameters and climate conditions to suggest the most suitable crops for your farm. The system analyzes:

  • Soil nutrients (N-P-K): The levels of Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium in your soil
  • Climate factors: Temperature, humidity, and rainfall in your area
  • Soil pH: The acidity or alkalinity of your soil

Based on these parameters, we recommend crops that are most likely to thrive in your specific conditions.
